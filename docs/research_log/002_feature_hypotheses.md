# 002 — Feature hypotheses

## Date
2026-06-18

## Hypotheses considered

### Short-term reversal
Recent 5-day underperformance may reverse because of liquidity shocks or temporary overreaction.

### Medium-term momentum
20–60 day price movement may persist because information diffuses gradually.

### Volatility / range
Recent volatility and intraday range may condition the signal. High-volatility names may have noisier continuation.

### Volume shock
Unusual volume may reflect attention or information arrival, but with OHLCV alone it is difficult to separate meaningful information from noise.

## Research decision
Keep features interpretable first. Avoid adding many weak features just to make the project look more complex.
