# 001 — Research question and scope

## Date
2026-06-18

## Question
Can public OHLCV-derived features produce medium-frequency cross-sectional ranking information for large-cap U.S. equities?

## Why this question
I wanted a research question that is small enough to implement reproducibly but close enough to real systematic research to demonstrate the process: signal definition, leakage control, validation, failure analysis, and portfolio diagnostics.

## Initial scope
- Public OHLCV only.
- Large-cap U.S. equities.
- 5–20 trading-day horizons.
- Cross-sectional target rather than absolute return target.

## Constraint
This is not an institutional-grade dataset. The value is in the research structure, not the strength of the dataset.
